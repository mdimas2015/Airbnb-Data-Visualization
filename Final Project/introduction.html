Listing a home on AirBnb in Los Angeles can be daunting when considering how competitive the market can get. As a host, you want to make sure you're making the right property acquisitions that will give you the highest return. Use our Superhost tool to explore Airbnb listings all over LA. You can see what neighborhoods have the highest and least concentrations of listings, and what kinds of listings exist there. For example, you may discover that a certain part of West Hollywood has many apartments and rooms, but not many private homes. Information like this can help you decide where to target your next property purchase. Once you get your property, you can use the map to see what prices for properties in your area are listed as to stay competitive.
